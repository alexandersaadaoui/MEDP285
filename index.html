Alexander Saadaoui

I create art in a variety of mediums to express my struggle, my frustrations, and the beauty of living. As an active New York City resident, I learned the experiences of those from all walks of life, different races, genders, the abled and disabled, young and old. Surrounding yourself with others that have distinct experiences strengthens your own experience and creativity. Through my writing, illustrations, editing, and videos, I communicate these experiences, in the hopes that others can see the world through my eyes.
